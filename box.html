  1. Grooming
    Cats are meticulous groomers. They often spend a significant amount of time cleaning themselves, which helps them stay clean and healthy. Regular grooming also reduces shedding and helps to keep their fur in good condition.
  2. independent
    Cats are known for their independent nature. They don't require constant attention and can entertain themselves for hours. This independence can be convenient for cat owners who have busy lifestyles.
  3. Litter Box Training
    Most cats are naturally inclined to use a litter box for their bathroom needs. Proper litter box training is one of the most appreciated habits of a cat, as it keeps your home clean and odor-free.
  4. Hunting Instinct
    Cats have a strong hunting instinct, and they often display this by "hunting" toys or playfully stalking objects. This behavior not only keeps them mentally and physically active but can also be entertaining for their human companions.
